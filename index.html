Can we see the Great Wall
of China from space?
The Great Wall of China can be seen from space with the
help of special cameras and lenses. The Great Wall, like
many other structures, is also visible when a shuttle is a
few hundred kilometers away from the Earth’s surface.

Why was Hadrian’s
Wall built?
Hadrian’s Wall, located in England, was built by
Roman Emperor Hadrian in the years ad 122–130. It is
believed that the wall was built to keep enemies of the
Roman Empire out of Britain. The wall also acted as a
checkpoint for traders who traveled across the borders
to conduct business, helping the emperor to collect
taxes and prevent the smuggling of goods.
